Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly ("we", "us", or "our") collects when you use our personal expense tracking application and related services (the "Service"), how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide the Service to you. This includes:

  • Account information — such as your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — the amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you choose to record.
  • Usage information — basic technical details, such as your browser type, that help us keep the Service running reliably.

We do not ask for, or intentionally collect, sensitive financial credentials such as bank logins or card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Display your spending history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • Secure your account and prevent unauthorized access or abuse.
  • Respond to your questions and support requests.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your expense information for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and retained only for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the Service. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, after which we will remove it except where retention is required by law.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on a small number of trusted third party providers — for example, hosting and infrastructure services — that help us operate the Service. These providers only process data on our behalf and are bound to handle it securely.

We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We may update this policy from time to time; when we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the Service after any changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
